My Introduction

I would like to welcome you to my small piece of the Internet. As a Information Technology professional for over 20+ years, my roles are vast from system administration and software development and support. My love of technology came to me as a youth, just wondering how things work and if I investigate the process can this piece become better. Today, my creative passions peek along with my professional maturity when I found web development. The Internet simply fascinates me. It allows me to entertain my techie and artsy side all in one. Professionally, I am a customer care analyst supporting warehouse software solutions. However, my web development studies at Wake Technical Community College have exposed me to multiple web technologies solutions: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MERN, WordPress, Joomla, PHP, and ASP.net MVC. I plan to remain technical by growing my web development skills and increasing my understanding of graphic design.
